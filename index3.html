I want to showcase my research skills with this writing. This one has my name on it, along with my good friend, Baishakhi Alam (who helped design this little portfolio you’re reading now). I spent a lot of time researching about Unmanned Aerial Vehicles (UAV), more commonly known as drones, while Baishakhi developed a routing algorithm for drone delivery services. We proposed that it would be theoretically possible to provide drone delivery services in Dhaka city, but we had to present our case in a well-written report.

While this writing may be quite technical for some, it has a succinct, yet informative background, well-documented presentation of other closely related studies on drone deliveries, a comprehensive description of replicating the study, and visually descriptive charts and tables to guide the reader through our findings. I included this writing because of how personal it is to me, from its inception to its execution, and because it showcases the rigor with which I am able to produce full-fledged reports.

15-minute read (~3000 words)
